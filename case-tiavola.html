The Challenge

Tiavola needed a high-performance, scalable Shopify Plus storefront with a custom section architecture that could grow alongside the brand's rapid expansion while maintaining a premium, consistent visual identity.

The Approach

I built and continue to maintain the entire frontend architecture from scratch. This involved developing a robust system of custom Shopify sections that empower the internal team to create unique pages without technical overhead.

  • Architected a performance-first Liquid theme with optimized asset delivery.
  • Developed a custom UI library within Shopify's section system.
  • Implemented advanced conversion-focused patterns for product discovery.
  • Ongoing optimization and maintenance of the Shopify Plus environment.

The Result

The result is a production-grade Shopify Plus storefront that serves as the brand's primary sales engine. The flexible section architecture has allowed the brand to launch new collections and campaigns with unprecedented speed.
