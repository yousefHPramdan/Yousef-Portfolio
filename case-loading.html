The Challenge

Loading-eg, a premium bag and luggage retailer, manages a massive multi-brand catalog including Arctic Hunter, RAHALA, and Golden Wolf. They needed a storefront that could handle thousands of SKUs with advanced filtering while integrating local Egyptian "Buy Now, Pay Later" (BNPL) services like valU.

The Approach

I focused on architecting a navigation and discovery system that could handle high-density data without overwhelming the user. This included a custom mega-menu and a localized checkout flow.

  • Built an advanced mega-menu for seamless navigation across multiple parent brands.
  • Implemented a complex collection filtering system for attributes like material, size, and brand.
  • Integrated localized BNPL (valU) installment calculators directly on product pages.
  • Dynamic low-stock indicators and scarcity UI to drive conversion on high-demand items.

The Result

The resulting storefront successfully handles a massive product catalog with a frictionless user journey. The integration of local fintech APIs has made premium products more accessible to the Egyptian market, driving significant growth in AOV.
