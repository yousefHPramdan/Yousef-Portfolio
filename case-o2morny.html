The Challenge

O2morny needed a dual-pronged solution: a highly polished storefront with complex multi-category navigation to showcase their diverse apparel lines, and a custom customer account experience that went beyond Shopify's default template limitations.

The Approach

I utilized a combination of advanced Liquid logic for the storefront and a React-based App Extension for the customer portal, ensuring a cohesive brand experience across all touchpoints.

  • Developed a custom mega-menu with GSAP-powered transitions for a fluid browsing experience.
  • Built advanced collection filtering tailored for high-volume apparel categories.
  • Developed a separate React Router-based customer account UI extension using Shopify Polaris.
  • Implemented a mobile-optimized drawer navigation for better handheld UX.

The Result

The solution provided O2morny with a high-end storefront that effectively organizes their large catalog. The custom React-based account portal has significantly improved customer satisfaction by providing a more intuitive and brand-aligned self-service experience.
