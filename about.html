The Developer Story

Merging Behavior
with Binary

From studying social structures to building high-performance Shopify storefronts.

Philosophy

Building for Humans,
Optimizing for Conversions.

My journey into development wasn't linear. With a background in Sociology, I spent years analyzing how people interact with systems, brands, and each other. When I discovered the world of e-commerce, I realized that a storefront isn't just a collection of code—it's a social environment.

Every millisecond of latency, every friction point in a checkout flow, and every confusing UI pattern is a barrier to human connection. My mission is to remove those barriers.

Today, I leverage that sociological lens combined with deep technical expertise in the Shopify Plus ecosystem to build stores that don't just look pretty—they perform.
