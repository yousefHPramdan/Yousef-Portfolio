# Product

## Register

brand

## Users
Potential clients, recruiters, and collaborators looking for a Front-End Shopify Developer.

## Product Purpose
A personal profile page showcasing Yousef Ramadan's work, projects, services, and pricing. The goal is to present a premium, cohesive, and compelling case to hire him for e-commerce and Shopify development.

## Brand Personality
Premium, Minimal, Bold. A balance of technical expertise and high-end design aesthetics with warmth and character.

## Anti-references
- Not a generic corporate site or mass-produced SaaS/WordPress/Bootstrap template.
- Not a basic resume page.
- Not overly playful, cluttered, chaotic, or confusing. No excessive animations.
- Not overly minimalist to the point of feeling empty/incomplete.
- Not a "hacker", "crypto/Web3", or heavy tech aesthetic (e.g., neon green terminal interfaces).

## Design Principles
- **Elevated Craft:** Every detail (typography, spacing, subtle interactions) must feel intentional and high-quality, reflecting the care put into premium Shopify themes.
- **Confident Minimalism:** Use whitespace and bold typography to communicate confidence without clutter.
- **Warmth in the Dark:** Maintain the dark theme but ensure it feels inviting and characterful, not stark or cold.
- **Focus on the Work:** The design should frame the portfolio pieces and services without distracting from them.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
Ensure high contrast ratios (especially with the dark background and gold accents), legible font sizes, and logical focus states for keyboard navigation. Animations should be respectful of reduced motion preferences.
