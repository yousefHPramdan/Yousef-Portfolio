The Challenge

Transform a robust Shopify Plus foundation into a bespoke, brand-specific experience that remains scalable and maintainable as the client grows.

The Approach

Leverage the Horizon architecture as a baseline and push it to the limits with Aceternity UI, delivering a modular, highly interactive storefront framework that can be branded rapidly for campaigns.

  • Establish a robust design token system and a reusable UI kit.
  • Integrate GSAP-driven interactions with a performant asset pipeline.
  • Provide a clear upgrade path for future brand-specific deployments.

The Result

A solid, adaptable platform that can be customized into premium, brand-specific storefronts with minimal lead time while preserving performance and accessibility.
